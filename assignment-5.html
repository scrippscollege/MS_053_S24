All the Daily Things (2018) by Saskia Freeke.

Moving Pattern

Objective

Design and code an animating moving pattern.

Description

For this assignment, you will design a new pattern by remixing patterns you've observed at the Seeing the Unseen: Math and Art exhibit.

Design Constraint

Canvas Size: flexible

Timeline

Due 2/24

  1. Identify mathematical concepts such as topology (Klein bottles, tori, etc.), geometry (tessellations, hyperbolic planes, projections, perspective, etc.), algebra (sequences, series, fractals, golden ratio, etc.), number theory (prime numbers, etc.). Include photos of your observations and explain how they correlate with specific mathematical concepts.
  2. On your notebook, design and sketch a new pattern by remixing the pattern(s) you've observed in the exhibition. What happens when you try to add motion to animate the pattern? Please attach a photo of your sketch and provide a brief explanation. We will work on re-creating and animating your pattern design in the upcoming week.

Due 3/6

  1. Begin working on your sketch. Incorporate For Loops and Nested For Loops to create the patterns. Then you will use IF / ELSE statements to animate the shapes.

Submission Guidelines

  1. Submit the sketch to Canvas Assignment 5
